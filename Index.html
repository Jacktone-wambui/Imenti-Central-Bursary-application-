About the Bursary Management System

Welcome to the IMETI-CENTRAL Bursary Management System! This platform serves as a bridge between generous donors and aspiring students within the IMETI-CENTRAL constituency. Here, donors have the opportunity to make a difference by providing donations to support educational opportunities in the constituency. Meanwhile, students from primary school to university level can register and apply for bursaries under the Constituency Development Fund (CDF). Our aim is to empower and uplift students by ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder their educational pursuits. Together, we can create a brighter future for the IMETI-CENTRAL constituency through accessible education and meaningful contributions. Join us today in making a positive impact in the lives of students and the community as a whole.
